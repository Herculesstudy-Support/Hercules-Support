Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I sign in?
    Enter your email and tap "Send code". You'll receive an 8-digit code via email. Enter the code to sign in.
  • I didn't receive the code
    Check your spam/junk folder. If still missing, wait 60 seconds and tap "Resend code". Avoid tapping multiple times quickly.
  • How do I track my study time?
    Go to the Study tab, select a subject, and tap "Start Session". The timer will track your study time.
  • How does the AI Coach work?
    The AI Coach generates practice questions, clutch cards, and study plans based on your VCE subjects. You can also add notes or photos for personalised questions.
  • How do I add friends?
    Go to Ranks > Friends tab and tap "Add friend by username". Your friend will receive a request they can accept.
  • How do I delete my account?
    Go to Profile, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete account" in the Danger Zone section.
